About Us

Mission:
ShikshaSarthi is an online Scholarship portal that aims at providing detailed information on all national and international Scholarship under one plateform. students can explore Scholarship based on their merid, cast, income, and study field in just a few clicks. Our aim is to create the next generaton of Scholarship websites that will enable students to find,search and compare Scholarship in a faster and more efficient way.

Vision:
We foster the youth to the pathway of excellence and helpe them to build and affluent career according to their potential. "Jab padhnega India!....Tabhi to badhega India!".

Team:
Scholarship is a team of thirty-year Engineering students :-Shubhangi Chouksey, Shreya Gupta, Yash Koshta, Shivansh Tiwari, Swapnil Jaiswar and Vishal Pratap Singh, who are trying to use their technical background to create Something innovative.
